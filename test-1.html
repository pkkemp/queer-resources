1st Dose - PFIZER - Midwest City Reed Ctr (Mar 10)

Please review the available vaccination slots below and click on the button to sign up.

ELIGIBILITY: This vaccination event is for any adult Oklahoma resident who meets one of the following criteria

  1. Will be 65 or older before 12/31/2021
  2. Any adult with severe comorbidities as defined by the Oklahoma State Department of Health (asthma, cancers, chronic lung disease, diabetes, chronic kidney disease, heart disease, high blood pressure/hypertension, obesity, Down syndrome, mellitus, or chronic liver disease)
  3. Any Adult at high risk of mortality and severe morbidity resulting from a COVID infection as determined by a physician
  4. K-12 Teachers or any staff working in a K-12 school
  5. Any adult eligible under other Phase 1, 2 or 3 criteria 

1ST DOSE: This clinic is for first dose vaccinations only. If you have already received a dose, please look for a second dose clinic. 

PFIZER: All vaccine available at this clinic will be Pfizer. If you would prefer the Moderna vaccine, please look for a first dose Moderna clinic. 
 

I understand that by scheduling a vaccine appointment I give my consent for the services that I am requesting from the Oklahoma City-County Health Department and its entities/contractors/partners. I understand that:

  1. At my appointment the risks and benefits for these services will be explained to me and that I will have the opportunity to ask questions.
  2. The information regarding myself and the services I receive will be entered into Oklahoma State Department of Health management information systems and may be used for program evaluation, management, and billing services.
  3. I may refuse service at any time.
  4. I will be required to fill out a consent form at the time of my vaccine.  If I refuse to fill out this consent form I will not be given a vaccine even if I have an appointment.

 

I acknowledge I will receive a copy of the manufacturers COVID-19 Face Sheet for Recipient and Caregivers prior to receiving the vaccine. Vaccine information statements (VIS) for Moderna, Pfizer, and Johnson & Johnson vaccines can be found on the agency website and also at:  www.modernatx.com/covid19vaccine-eua or www.cvdvaccine.com or https://www.janssenlabels.com/emergency-use-authorization/Janssen+COVID-19+Vaccine-Recipient-fact-sheet.pdf 

Date: 03/10/2021 (Wed.)
